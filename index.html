About Real Life International

Real Life International is a global news and information platform dedicated to reporting major world events, political developments, economic trends, technological progress, and historical events that have shaped our world.

Our goal is to provide readers with reliable information and clear understanding of the global forces that influence nations and societies. We combine news, analysis, and historical education to help readers stay informed about both current events and the past events that explain today's world.

In addition to breaking news, Real Life International explores major historical moments such as the World Wars, the American Revolution, and other global conflicts and turning points that changed history. Through detailed articles and educational content, our platform helps readers understand the deeper context behind major global developments.

Real Life International believes that knowledge empowers people. By providing accurate reporting, thoughtful analysis, and historical insight, we aim to inform, educate, and inspire readers across the world.

History Articles

World War I – How the Great War Began and Ended

World War I began in 1914 after the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand. The conflict involved major world powers and lasted until 1918, reshaping Europe and the global political order.

World War II – The Largest Conflict in Human History

World War II began in 1939 when Germany invaded Poland. The war involved nations across the globe and ended in 1945 after the defeat of Nazi Germany and Imperial Japan.

American Revolution – The Birth of the United States

The American Revolution (1775-1783) was the struggle of the thirteen American colonies to gain independence from Britain, leading to the creation of the United States of America.
